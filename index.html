About

Mach Learn

SIGNAL is the signature and most anticipated flagship event of IEEE SPS Kerala Chapter co-hosted by Viswajyothi College of Engineering and Technology (VJCET) from 13th May 2022- to 15th May 2022. The 3-day extravaganza is filled with amazing speakers, networking sessions, and cultural nights. Students from various Student Branches can come together to foster technological innovation and discover new ideas. The event will be the hub of networking, relationship building, and learning. The event includes inspiring keynotes and uses case-focused sessions to help you meet today’s technology challenges while preparing for tomorrow’s opportunities. Moreover, it connects you to renowned personalities in the respective field of Engineering & Technology.

Meet Our

Co-Host

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering Kothamangalam

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by Mar Athanasius College Association, Aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was first in Asia under Christian Management. The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It now has six full-fledged departments apart from the two auxiliary departments of Mathematics and Science and Humanities.
